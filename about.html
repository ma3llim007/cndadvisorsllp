About us

About Us

C&D group has a rich legacy dating back to 1964, founded by the Late Shri S.P. Chhajed, Past President of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). For over six decades, the group has been a trusted provider of a wide range of professional services, including Assurance, Tax Advisory and Litigation, and Management Consultancy.

Our core leadership along with the calibre of 200+ dynamic team of professionals have made us one of the most preferred firm in India and Globally.

We have grown multi fold over the years due to our business acumen, regular updation on industry trends and deep understanding of the client’s requirement.

C&D Advisors LLP is a firm engaged in IT Audit and Advisory Services.

Our mission is to develop innovative and comprehensive IT Audit Solutions for the client.
