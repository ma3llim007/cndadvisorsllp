Segregation of Duties (SoD)

  • Understanding of Organizational Roles and Responsibility Matrix alongwith Job responsibilities.

  • Understanding of the User Access Review (UAR) mechanism in place at the organization, including follow up activities post review.

  • Understanding the application / tool used for User Access Review.

  • Understanding of the access provided to the in-scope application.

  • Identifying the gaps vis-à-vis roles and responsibilities.

  • Advising the client for the gaps identified and providing solutions.

  • Identifying mitigating controls where the identified gaps cannot be addressed.
